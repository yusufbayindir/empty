## App Description (4,000 chars max)

**Where your money comes home.**

Managing money as a couple just got beautiful. Hearth combines both partners' finances into one shared view — with privacy controls that let each of you share exactly what you're comfortable sharing.

**BUILT FOR COUPLES**
See your combined accounts side by side. Know your real household net worth instantly. Understand where you're spending together — and where you're spending apart.

**PRIVACY ON YOUR TERMS**
Every account has three visibility levels: Full (your partner sees everything), Balance Only (they see the number, not the transactions), or Hidden (just you). Change anytime, no questions asked.

**AI COACH THAT GETS YOU**
Hearth's AI coach surfaces what matters: overspending alerts before you overshoot, subscription audits to find forgotten charges, goal milestone celebrations, and personalized tips built around both partners' actual behavior — not generic advice.

**SHARED GOALS, REAL PROGRESS**
Set savings goals together — a dream vacation, a home down payment, an emergency fund — and watch both contributions build toward it in real time. Each partner's share is tracked separately with a beautiful dual-progress bar.

**SPLIT EXPENSES FAIRLY**
No more awkward "who pays for this?" moments. Mark any expense as split and set the exact share for each partner. The app keeps track so you both stay in sync.

**MONEY DATE MODE**
Set aside 15 minutes together with Hearth's guided Money Date: a curated agenda that walks you through monthly spending, goal progress, AI insights, and upcoming bills — all in one beautiful session.

**SUBSCRIPTION INTELLIGENCE**
Find every active subscription across both partners' accounts. See exactly what you're paying, spot duplicates, and decide together what's worth keeping.

**UPCOMING BILLS, ALWAYS COVERED**
See every bill due in the next 30 days. Each partner can check off their confirmation — no more "did you pay the internet?" texts.

**WHAT HEARTH TRACKS**
• Checking, savings, credit, and investment accounts
• 16 spending categories with per-category trend charts
• Monthly spending reports with category breakdowns
• Net worth across all connected accounts

**HEARTH PREMIUM — 14-DAY FREE TRIAL**
Unlimited account connections, AI Coach insights, split expense tracking, shared goals, monthly reports, and bill reminders. No commitment required during trial.
